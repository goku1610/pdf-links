Abakkus Diversified Alpha Fund Cat III AIF strongly believes in the pursuit of generating alpha over merely aligning with the benchmark index. The objective is to outperform the average market returns.

Their focus is on growth companies, especially those whose profitability prospects are anticipated to surpass the market average.

They prioritize fundamentally underpriced stocks that present promising growth potential.

The fund also values mid-cap companies, particularly those with scalable business models that possess the potential to evolve into large-cap entities.

Abakkus Diversified Alpha Fund Cat III AIF’s approach is rooted in bottom-up research with a special emphasis on the balance sheet, signaling its commitment to sound financials.

They believe that numbers and hard data are more telling than presentations and promotional hype.

At its core, the fund believes that the ultimate metric of a successful investment is the earnings it generates.

Abakkus Diversified Alpha Fund Cat III AIF is risk-conscious and firmly believes that expected returns should be commensurate with the uncertainties and risks undertaken.

They hold the view that a good company doesn’t automatically translate to a good stock.

The focus is also on the price they pay for an investment and the value that is subsequently derived from it. They constantly assess the inherent value in the price of a stock.

Abakkus Diversified Alpha Fund Cat III AIF subscribes to a buy-and-hold strategy, reflecting its long-term investment outlook.

They approach stock investments with the mindset of investing in a business, underlining their commitment to the intrinsic value of the enterprise.

The fund thinks like a partner in the businesses they invest in, implying a deeper engagement and understanding of the companies.

Abakkus Diversified Alpha Fund Cat III AIF adopts a dynamic approach to investing, where each opportunity is assessed on its individual merit without a one-size-fits-all lens.

They are not bound by any specific investment theme or style, allowing for a diverse portfolio construction.
