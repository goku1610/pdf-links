ASK Group is a renowned financial services group and a leading industry player in wealth and investment management, catering to some of the most affluent families in India. ASK Emerging Opportunities Portfolio or ASK EOP PMS is designed to build a concentrated portfolio of quality businesses across range of market capitalization (Large, Mid and Small cap) that have superior long-term compounding potential.

The two core objectives of this ASK Emerging Opportunities Portfolio or ASK EOP PMS are: Capital Preservation and Capital Appreciation over a period of time. This is achieved through long-term investments in high-quality companies with strong growth prospects.
