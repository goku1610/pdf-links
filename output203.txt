ASK Investment Managers Limited (ASK IM) stands as a prominent asset and wealth management company, with its primary focus on serving the High Net Worth Individual (HNI) and Ultra High Net Worth Individual (UHNI) segments within the Indian market. ASKIM holds the distinction of being among the first companies to secure a Portfolio Management Services license in India and is presently one of the leading providers of Discretionary Equity Portfolio Management Services. Their investment focus is exclusively directed towards listed Indian equities, serving clients domiciled in India as well as offshore investors through segregated accounts and commingled funds.

ASK Growth Portfolio is one of the industry’s first PMS Investment Approach launched way back in Jan 2001.

ASK Growth Portfolio is designed to provide medium to long-term returns, by seeking to buy growth at value prices from a diversified portfolio of Indian equities with favorable long term prospects. This portfolio is an ideal investment for investors who would like to participate in India’s growth opportunity.

The two core objectives of this ASK Growth Portfolio are: Capital Preservation and Capital Appreciation over a period of time. This is achieved through long-term investments in high-quality companies with strong growth prospects.

Their investment strategy involves selecting Indian equities associated with high-quality businesses led by exceptional management teams, exhibiting sustained long-term growth potential at reasonable and equitable valuations.
